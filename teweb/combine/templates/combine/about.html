About Tellurium Web

Tellurium Web provides an online service for the execution of CombineArchives. The current version is {{ commit }} and supports:

  • model descriptions in SBML
  • simulation descriptions in SED-ML

Does this service store your SBML model or file?

This service is implemented as a server-side facility, meaning that the computations are physically performed on our computers, which are housed at the Humboldt University. As a consequence, your Combine Archive file must be transmitted to, and stored temporarily on, our servers in order to process it. In the event that this service itself exhibits an error, the Tellurium Web Team may need to inspect the material you submitted (i.e., the Combine Archive file) to ascertain the cause of the error. Your Combine Archive file will never be passed willingly to any third party, and it will be deleted after a period of time. Your Combine Archive file will only be used for the purposes of validating it, for gathering general statistics about the uses of this service and Combine Archives and SED-ML in general, and for debugging the service itself (and then, only if necessary). By submitting content to this service, you grant the Tellurium Web Team the right to use and store your file temporarily for these purposes.
